What this does
  • Downloads htdemucs_ft_vocals_fp16weights.onnx (~166 MB) from HF on first run. The browser caches it forever.
  • Chunks long inputs into 7.8 s segments with 25% overlap and runs them sequentially.
  • Reassembles the vocals stem and produces a 16-bit PCM WAV at the original sample rate.
  • Source: examples/browser/index.html in the demucs-onnx repo.
